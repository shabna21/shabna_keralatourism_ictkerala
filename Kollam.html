Kollam

Kollam, known by its former name Quilon, and Desinganadu, is an old seaport and city on the Laccadive Sea coast of the Indian state of Kerala. It is situated 66 kilometers north of the state capital Trivandrum. The city is on the banks of Ashtamudi Lake. Ashtamudi Lake, Thangassery lighthouse, Kollam Beach, Thirumullavaram Beach, Kollam Adventure Park, Anandavalleswaram Temple, Sri Mahaganapathi Temple, Kumbhavurutty Waterfalls, Palaruvi Falls, Thevally Palace and many more.
